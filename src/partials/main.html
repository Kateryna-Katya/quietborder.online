BORDER BOUND

Easy to Learn. Hard to Master.

So we had this ridiculously simple idea for Border Bound that somehow turns your brain inside out (in a good way, I promise).
Fill every box on the grid with the color of its border.
Special boxes introduce new ways to manipulate color across the board — spreading, redirecting, and transforming tiles in ways that require careful planning and precise thinking.
There are no timers.
No distractions.
Just pure logic and elegant design.

Plan carefully. Think deeply. Master the grid.

HOW IT WORKS

A Simple Concept with Strategic Depth

Each puzzle presents a grid of boxes bordered by color. Your objective:

  • Make every box match its surrounding border color.

  • To achieve this, you use special interactive tiles that influence the board in unique ways.

  • Some tiles spread color in one direction.

  • Others affect nearby tiles.

  • Some introduce entirely new transformation rules.

You'll think it's easy at first, but trust me - things get complicated fast.
